Valley Rowing Center

Youth Rowing in the scenic Matanuska Valley

Mission

Valley Rowing Center was founded in 2023 with the goal

of bringing youth rowing to the Matanuska-Susitna Valley and promote the growth of rowing in Alaska

Rowing is an endurance, team sport that that promotes fitness, self disipline and camaraderie.

We strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels like they belong.

Are coaches are well trained and we hope to help are athletes along their rowing journey.
